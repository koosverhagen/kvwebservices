Equine Transport UK — Custom Horsebox Hire Booking Platform

A custom website, booking flow and protected admin system for a horsebox hire business that needed more practical control over bookings, payments, deposits, customer records and day-to-day admin.

Custom Booking Flow · Admin Dashboard · Stripe Payments · Customer Records

The Challenge

Equine Transport UK needed more than a standard website. The business needed a practical system for self-drive horsebox hire, driven services, availability, payments, deposits, customer details and booking management.

The aim was to reduce manual admin, make customer booking clearer, and give the business a reliable admin area for everyday use.

What Was Built

  • New public website for the hire and transport service
  • Custom booking flow built around the business workflow
  • Protected admin dashboard for managing bookings
  • Customer records and booking history
  • Availability calendar for lorries and hire periods
  • Stripe payment links, deposits, balances and refunds
  • Data migration from older booking tools
  • Mobile-friendly admin layout for day-to-day use

Public Booking Flow

Customers can view the service, choose the right type of hire, check the relevant information and start a booking through a clear public flow. The booking journey was designed around real horsebox hire requirements, not a generic template.

Protected Admin System

A private admin area was built for managing bookings, customers, availability, payments and day-to-day actions. The admin tools are laid out for practical use, including mobile-friendly booking cards and clear actions for common tasks.

Payments and Deposits

The platform supports payment tracking for hire totals, outstanding balances, deposits and refunds. This gives the business a clearer view of what has been paid, what is still outstanding, and what needs follow-up.

Customer and Booking Management

Customer details, booking records, booking dates, lorry information, payment status and admin notes are kept together so the business has one practical place to manage each booking.

Data Migration

Existing customers, bookings and payments were reviewed and moved into the new setup. This helped preserve important business history while making the new admin system more useful from launch.

Mobile Admin Design

The admin experience was designed to work well on phones and tablets, because real booking admin often happens away from a desk. Booking cards, calendar views and key actions were kept clear and practical for daily use.

Outcome

KV Web Services built a custom booking platform for Equine Transport UK, not just a simple website or a basic connection between third-party tools. The result is a practical business system that combines the public website, booking flow, admin tools, customer records, payment tracking and availability management in one setup.
