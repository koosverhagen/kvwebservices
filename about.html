About KV Web Services

KV Web Services creates high-performance websites and practical custom business systems for small UK businesses that need reliable online tools, not unnecessary complexity.

Projects are built around the way the business actually works — from clear public pages to booking flows, admin dashboards, customer records and payment tracking where needed.

UK-based, working with clients nationwide.

What I Do

  • Design and build modern business websites aligned to commercial goals
  • Build practical custom booking systems with admin dashboards and customer records
  • Deliver integrations for payments, bookings, forms, and third-party tools
  • Optimise speed, user experience, and search visibility for sustainable growth

How I Work

  • Discovery: clarify goals, priorities, workflow and project scope
  • Build: implement a clean, responsive experience with the right practical tools
  • Launch: validate quality across devices and browsers before release
  • Support: provide ongoing refinements, updates, and technical guidance

Why Clients Choose KV Web Services

  • Direct, transparent communication with practical, no-jargon guidance
  • Custom development that prioritises quality over quick template shortcuts
  • Practical business tools designed to reduce manual admin
  • A performance-first mindset focused on reducing friction and improving conversion
  • Reliable delivery with clear next steps at every stage
