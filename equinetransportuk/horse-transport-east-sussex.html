Questions people ask before booking horse transport

Who offers horse transport in East Sussex?

Equine Transport UK offers self-drive horsebox hire and driven horse transport from Upper Broadreed Farm, Five Ashes, East Sussex.

Can I hire a 3.5T horsebox?

Yes. We offer 3.5 tonne self-drive horsebox hire, including half-day options where available under the booking rules.

Can I hire a 7.5T horse lorry?

Yes. We offer 7.5 tonne horse lorry hire, including layouts for larger journeys and overnight-style use where appropriate.

Yes. Our driven horse transport service is available by quote for customers who do not want to self-drive.
