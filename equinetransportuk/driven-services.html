Driven transport FAQs

Common driven horse transport questions

Can you transport my horse if I do not want to drive?

Yes. The driven service is designed for customers who want Equine Transport UK to carry out the journey rather than hiring a lorry for self-drive use.

Is the online price a confirmed quote?

No. The calculator gives a guide estimate based on route distance and journey details. We confirm the final price with you directly.

What journeys can you help with?

We can help with shows, vet appointments, clinics, yard moves, sales, purchases, local journeys and planned long-distance horse transport across the UK.

Where are you based?

Equine Transport UK is based at Upper Broadreed Farm, Stonehurst Lane, Five Ashes, East Sussex TN20 6LL. Driven horse transport is available across the whole of the UK.
