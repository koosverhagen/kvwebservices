Booking Payment & Deposit

At the time of booking, a booking payment of £75 for 3.5T or £100 for 7.5T is required. This payment is non-refundable but may be transferred to a future booking if sufficient notice is given.

In addition, a £200 security deposit is required for all vehicles. This is taken as a pre-authorisation/card hold, not an immediate payment. Funds are reserved on your card and will be released after the hire, or partially/fully captured only if required under the hire agreement, for example damage, excessive cleaning, fuel or late return charges.

Cancellations & Refunds

  • If a booking is cancelled more than 48 hours before the start of hire, a full refund of all monies paid will be issued.
  • If a booking is cancelled within 48 hours of the hire start time, the initial booking payment of £75 for 3.5T or £100 for 7.5T will not be refunded.
  • This amount will be carried over to a future booking as a credit. A voucher will be issued to the customer and may be redeemed against a future hire, subject to availability.

Driver & Questionnaire Requirements

  • First-time hirers, or customers who have not hired within the last 3 months, must complete the Millens Hire Questionnaire LONG Form.
  • Customers who have hired within the last 3 months may complete the Millens Hire Questionnaire SHORT Form.

What’s Included — 3.5 Tonne

  • 24-hour Equine Rescue Service
  • CCTV and aluminium flooring
  • Regularly serviced and safety-checked vehicles
  • Fully comprehensive insurance for drivers aged 22–70
  • Vehicle supplied with a full tank of diesel
  • Flexible hire service
  • Open 7 days a week, all year round

Fuel & Mileage

  • Fuel level must match on return. A £50 charge plus the cost of fuel will apply if the vehicle is not returned at the same level.
  • A maximum of 300 miles per paid day is included. Mileage above this allowance will be charged at £0.30 per mile.

Late Return Policy

Vehicles must be returned at the agreed time. Returns more than 30 minutes late may incur a £50 late return charge, unless a valid reason has been communicated to us at the time the delay occurs, not retrospectively.

Insurance & Liability

  • The insurance excess is £400.
  • Vehicles or cars left at our premises are left entirely at the hirer’s own risk.

Emissions & Road Charges

Hirers are responsible for any emission zone, congestion charge, clean air zone or similar fees incurred during the hire period.

Additional Services

Vehicle delivery and collection is charged at £1 per mile, calculated at four times the distance from TN20 6LL to your address when both delivery and collection are required.
