Directions

If coming from Royal Tunbridge Wells

Proceed on the A267 towards Mayfield. Just before Mayfield is a roundabout, take second exit towards Heathfield. After about two miles there is a turning on your right to Rotherfield and directly next to that a turning to Skipper Hill School. Take the turning to the school. After another 500 yards take the first turning on the right into Stonehurst Lane. After approx. 1.5 miles you go over a little white bridge and start to go up hill. We are the next house on the right and the yard is directly after the house. Please come to the yard.

If coming down the A22 towards Eastbourne

Following the A22 towards Eastbourne take the turning onto the A272 towards Buxted and Heathfield. Follow the A272 until you come out of Buxted. As you exit Buxted, turn left into Five Chimneys Lane, signposted to Rotherfield and Crowborough, and continue to the end. Continue straight across into Stocklands Lane to end, then left into Stonehurst Lane. We are the first house on the left after the Kit Wilson rescue home and the yard is just before the house. Please come to the yard.
